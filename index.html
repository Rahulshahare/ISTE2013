On 18 th & 19 th March'13

INNOVISION  2k13  is a national symposium that is proudly being organized in R.C.E.R.T. ,Chandrapur , Maharashtra under  I.S.T.E. (INDIAN SOCIETY FOR  TECHNICAL EDUCATION).
Its basically a students  technical chapter that is well famous for its technical events. Carrying out the tradition like every year , even this year , we are organizing some very popular and innovative technical events under INNOVISION  2k13.  Therefore for the participants its an excellent benchmark for showcasing your clever  technical skills and your competence attitude.

PULSE 2k10, HORIZO , INNOVISION 2k12 etc had already written  our success story in the past years . So now gear up to participate and engulf yourself in the spirit of technology and flavor of competence and be a part of INNOVISION  2k13 .
