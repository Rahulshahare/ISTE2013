On 18 th & 19 th March'13

" The Scavenger Hunt "

Notification : The online registration for scavenger hunt is not available, so you must register on the spot.

The game is nothing but simply a race of mind, cleverness and shrewdness. In this game participants have to register themselves, compete , solve the puzzles, showcase your coordination and collectively complete each of their challenges.

The number of teams participating will be 18 maximum.

Rules:-

  • And these teams will totally be shortlisted on the basis of first come first take.
  • Each team will be comprising of max. 4 participants.
  • The members of a team can comprise of students from any department any year what so ever.
  • The game is divided in basically two rounds.
  • The first round comprises of two slots and second round is the ultimate finale.
  • Each team comprises of maximum 4 members.
  • 7 teams from different departments and 2 teams from first year will simultaneously remain active during the first slot of the round and similarly the rest  9 teams in the second slot respectively
  • Out of those two particular slots the winner and runner up from both the slots will be short listed.
  • Now in the second and finale round these top 4 teams will be competing against each other and at the end there will one single winner and a runner up.

The main parameters of the game:

  • The hunt for reaching the destination would consist of 5 clues each.
  • At the starting location each team would be provided with the initial clue that will direct them towards the starting location.
  • The next clue would be present at that location itself and in the similar way the chain goes on.
  • The first team reaching the destination will be declared as the winner and runner up consecutively.
  • A fixed time will be alloted within which the team has to complete their respective puzzles & reach their final destination.

Some strict instructions to be obeyed :

  • While a team is in motion i.e. when its playing the game, use of cellphones or any other electronic gadgets is strictly prohibited
  • During the course of play the participants are strictly not allowed to talk to any outsiders.
  • Disobeying any of the above rules will lead to disqualifying of the respective team.

Entry fees : Rs. 200 Per team.
