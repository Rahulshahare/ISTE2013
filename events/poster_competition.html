On 18 th & 19 th March'13

            “ Nirbhaya ”

Theme:  Women Safety in India.

Description: Issues related to women’s in India. E.g. : Domestic Violence, Female Feticide, Sexual Molestation, Women’s Strength, Women’s and Society, women’s culture etc.

Rules for poster competition:

  • Posters will measure 560 x 380 (size in mm).
  • Posters must be the original design and creation of the entrant(s).
  • Use of computer graphics is restricted.
  • All entries become the property of the ISTE and cannot be returned.
  • Entry should be sent on or before 15 March 2013 till 5 pm.
  • All the poster shall be submitted to Vineet Singh(T.L. Poster) on 17th March from 10am to 5pm.
  • All the participants shall be awarded with Certificates of appreciation.
  • Drawing must convey the given theme.
  • Do not put any student identification on front of submission.
  • Put your registration number on back side.

Students may work individually or in teams. , the maximum number of students per team is 3.

Entry fees : Rs. 150 per team .

For more information contact :

  • Vineet singh (9421003636)
  • Mohnish soni (8983228102)
