On 18 th & 19 th March'13

"Circunics (MakeThat Circuit)"


Problem Statement :


You have to design a specific electronics application by using the components provided by us. The application will use the concepts from basic Analog and Digital electronics and to be designed on a Bread Board.

Event Rules :


  • Teams of two participants only can contest for this event.
  • Candidates should bring their own Multimeters.
  • There will be total 2 rounds.
  • First round will be elimination round with ON SPOT PROBLEM STATEMENT from fields of electronics(555 timer IC & LM324 Comparator IC).
  • Second round will be circuit designing round in which actual application has to be made(Based on 555 timer IC & LM324 Comparator IC).
  • Both the rounds are open in nature, means you will be provided question sheets/components on the registration desk and you need to submit them back within the specified time limit.
  • There will be time bonus.
  • All the components and material required for the circuit will be provided by us.
  • Power supply and other testing devices like CRO (if needed) and other tools will be made available in a shared hall.

 

CIRCUIT : (Disclosed on time)

 

The application will be judged according to the following criteria:

  • Design of circuit. (10)
  • Compactness of design. (15)
  • Efficiency of the application. (15)
  • Desired output. (20)
  • Exploration of concepts/theories.(10)
  • Breadboarding skills/neatness of the implemented circuit. (10)
  • Additional functions/options available in the Application. (10)
  • Contents of Chart Sheet. (10)

Entry fee : Rs.250
