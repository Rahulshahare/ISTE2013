On 18 th & 19 th March'13

“Scrap Art (Best Of Waste) "

Description:

The participants are expected to make use of all the junk they are given with and come up with something extraordinary or simple. (The choice has always been yours). The teams battling are expected to pit out their creations against each other and the best of the creations will have to eventually be singled out.
Format:
The teams are provided with scrap of all sorts and are expected to make something out of it within the stipulated time. The best of the lot is eventually declared the winner. The criteria for evaluation include the finishing, perfection, working of the creation, and the innovation. So why wait? Venture into this scrap head challenge and emerge out as the winner.

Topics:

  • WINDMILL / TOWERS
  • AEROPLANE / AIRCRAFT
  • CAAPULT

Rules:

  • Every group will be given 45 seconds to collect desired material from the junk poolCutting tools will also be provided.
  • Use of articles other than those provided by organizers will lead to disqualification.
  • The model will have to be completed in the stipulated time. No extra time will be given. Time duration will be given on the spot.
  • The call of the judges is final and no further argument will be entertained.

TOPICS:

         
  • Aeroplane/Aircraft
  • Towers or Windmill
  • Catapult

A team can have a maximum of 4 members

Registration fee Rs 200/- per goup
