On 18 th & 19 th March'13

"Ad-Mad"

Description :


If you want to make your presence felt here, all that you need is to show your capability to give a smart advertising for anything weird given out to you. Be it a car which can have only the reverse gear or a mobile phone which can even wash your clothes and even iron it (Folks! Purely imaginary, you name it! We have got it here). Be it anything – Your sole responsibility is to make this worthless product marketable in your own way. The participants should have to cast a spell on the buyers and dare to express it smart enough to move their throb.
So feel free to give it a try.

Entry fee :   200 Rs/ Team.

 

Topics For Ad-Mad

  • Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan
  • Save the girl child
  • Cosmetics / Deo. / Perfumes
  • Mobile / Laptops
  • Tourists places / Travels services
  • Pain relief (Balm, cream, Iodex)
  • Washing / bathing soaps
  • Service provider (Airtel, idea, BSNL, TATA sky etc.)
  • Marriage bureau
  • LIC, Bank, Industry, University, Organisation
  • Automobiles

Participants : Maximum of  4 members in a team.

 

Rules:

  • Participants will have to produce advertisement for a product in a proper way.
  • Time to present the ad will be 3-4 minutes.
  • Presentation of the ad can be given in any scheduled language.
  • Ad must not contain any obscene words. Any sort of matter which is conflicting to any culture/ language / religion.
  • Judging will be done on the basis of originality, humour and stage presence.
  • Negative marking for non abidance of rules.
  • Judges decision will be final. 

Entry Fee : Rs. 200 per team.
